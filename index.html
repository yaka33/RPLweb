A Lightweight Trust Management Framework in
the Networking Layer of IoT Devices
25_26J_009

Exploring the frontiers of technology to solve real-world problems with cutting-edge solutions.

Project Abstract

This research presents a lightweight trust management framework designed to enhance secure and reliable data transmission in IoT networks. In IoT environments, data is often forwarded through multiple intermediate nodes, which may behave maliciously or unreliably due to resource constraints and dynamic network conditions.

To address this issue, the proposed solution introduces a Trust Computation Engine (TCE) that evaluates node behavior using multiple lightweight metrics such as residual energy, historical observations, trust accuracy, and environmental conditions. These metrics are combined using an adaptive model to dynamically calculate trust values.

The framework integrates trust into RPL-based routing decisions, enabling the network to avoid unreliable nodes and improve communication reliability. Additionally, an energy-aware scheduling mechanism is used to optimize trust computation frequency, ensuring minimal overhead for resource-constrained devices.

The system is implemented and evaluated using Contiki-NG and the Cooja simulator, measuring key performance indicators such as Packet Delivery Ratio (PDR), latency, and energy consumption. The results demonstrate improved network performance and security compared to traditional approaches.

Overview

The Vision

To create a secure, trustworthy, and efficient IoT communication environment where devices can exchange data reliably without being affected by malicious or unreliable nodes. The vision is to enable smart systems (such as smart cities, healthcare, and industrial IoT) to operate with high reliability and minimal risk.

The Mission

To design and implement a lightweight and adaptive trust management framework that enhances IoT network security while maintaining low energy consumption. The mission focuses on integrating trust evaluation into routing mechanisms to ensure efficient, scalable, and secure communication in resource-constrained environments.

Key Impact

  • Improves data reliability and integrity in IoT networks
  • Detects and avoids malicious or faulty nodes
  • Enhances routing efficiency and decision-making
  • Reduces energy consumption through adaptive mechanisms
  • Supports development of secure and scalable IoT applications
